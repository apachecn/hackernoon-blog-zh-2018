## [***How the West got China wrong —***](https://www.economist.com/news/leaders/21737517-it-bet-china-would-head-towards-democracy-and-market-economy-gamble-has-failed-how)

“[Seems] China stepped from autocracy into dictatorship…when Xi Jinping, already the world’s most powerful man, let it be known that he will change China’s constitution so that he can rule as president for as long as he chooses….

*After the collapse of the Soviet Union…Western leaders believed that giving China a stake in institutions such as the [WTO]…that economic integration would encourage China to evolve into a market economy and that, as they grew wealthier, its people would come to yearn for democratic freedoms, rights and the rule of law***….**

**[Instead] Xi has steered…towards repression, state control and confrontation…purging potential rivals [and]…imprisoning free-thinking lawyers and…criticism of the party and the government in the media and online…creating a surveillance state**….

***[Yet] it holds its authoritarian system up as a rival to liberal democracy…’and a Chinese approach to solving the problems facing mankind’***….

*China has been integrated into the global economy…[but] is not a market economy and, on its present course, never will be.* **Instead, it increasingly controls business as an arm of state power. It sees a vast range of industries as strategic…[with foreign] commerce always seems to be on China’s terms….**

**China embraces some Western rules, but also seems to be drafting a parallel system of its own…[like] Belt and Road Initiative, which promises to invest over $1tn in markets abroad, ultimately dwarfing the Marshall plan…[that] creates a Chinese-funded web of influence…[using] business to confront its enemies…[as] a complement to the hard power of armed force…bent on driving America out of East Asia*….***

***【奇怪的是】美国一直不愿意或者不能阻止它……【并且】输掉了对中国的赌注，就在它自己的民主国家正遭受信任危机的时候****……[特朗普]承诺“让美国再次伟大”有点退回到单边主义的味道，这只会加强中国的力量。*

西方不情愿地容忍中国的弊端越久，以后挑战它们就越危险。在位的和崛起的超级大国之间的竞争……增加了破坏性不稳定的可能性。"[https://www . economist . com/news/leaders/21737517-it-bet-China-would-head to-democracy-and-market-economy-gamble-failed-how](https://www.economist.com/news/leaders/21737517-it-bet-china-would-head-towards-democracy-and-market-economy-gamble-has-failed-how)

## [**合成生命体的设计可能成为一个新产业—**](https://www.economist.com/news/science-and-technology/21737495-design-synthetic-lifeforms-could-become-new-industry-robotic-labs)

“[DNA]生物基地正在世界各地建立起来……[进行]基因工程研究……[并]在一个上午按照所需的精确浓度混合 15 万份 DNA 样本。

**起点……是一个[部分]…[或]基因代码片段的库，不同的基因“回路”可以从这里组装起来**。用生物技术的话来说，一个回路是一组基因的集合，它们为了一个共同的目标一起工作……[然后]组装成称为质粒的环状 DNA 分子。为了获得合适的质粒，代工厂的客户可以简单地从库中订购零件……[或者]提供他们自己的专有片段……[*全部]它们的内容自动混合。*

**整个过程由……客户提供的计算机代码控制，该代码描述了…[然后]引入活细胞——细菌或酵母……的实验，并对结果进行测试，以查看哪个电路(如果有的话)如预期那样运行。[铸造厂]一天可以制造和测试大约 15，000 种不同的基因设计……储存完整的细胞系，供以后测试，或者从中培养组织用于测试。**

*【最终】在其他行业司空见惯的标准将开始在生物基金会中出现……【使得】设计新的合成生命形式的过程更容易从……现在的小企业扩大到更像是一个全球性行业。*"[https://www . economist . com/news/science-and-technology/21737495-design-synthetic-life forms-can-been-new-industry-robotic-labs](https://www.economist.com/news/science-and-technology/21737495-design-synthetic-lifeforms-could-become-new-industry-robotic-labs)
